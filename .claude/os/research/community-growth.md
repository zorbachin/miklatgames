# Research: community-first growth + institutions + holidays (sourced)

## Viral case studies (channel → numbers)
- **Wordle**: 90 players (Nov 2021) → 300K (Jan 2) → 2M+ a week later. Trigger:
  emoji-grid share (Dec 2021); 1.2M results tweeted Jan 1–13; shared heavily in
  WhatsApp/group chats. NYT bought it for "low seven figures," brought NYT
  "tens of millions" of users.
- **Among Us**: flopped 2 years (~1,800 CCU) → Twitch streamer chain (Korean
  streamers → Sodapoppin Jul 15 2020) → 60M DAU by Sept, ~500M MAU Nov 2020,
  264M downloads in 2020. Lesson: the game didn't change; the channel did.
- **Vampire Survivors**: solo dev, ~£1,100 budget, free web build first →
  YouTubers (Northernlion Jan 2022) → 1M copies in ~3 months → dev worth ~£40M.
- **Friday Night Funkin'**: free on Newgrounds (community hub) → most-viewed
  submission ever (83M) → $2.25M Kickstarter.
- **Hamster Kombat**: 0 → 300M players <5 months inside Telegram. Platform-native
  distribution is the whole game.
- **Stumble Guys**: 9-person team → 225M+ downloads, $40M IAP (TikTok-kids
  virality asserted, unsourced split — flag).
- **Hades**: 700K of first 1M copies sold DURING early access — community as
  co-developer.

## Teacher/institution-spread products (the Miklat schools playbook)
- **Kahoot**: 47% of US K-12 teachers by 2017, 70M MAU, now 1B+ players/yr.
- **Prodigy Math**: 3K → 100M+ users in ~9y with "little-to-no marketing" —
  pure teacher-to-teacher spread.
- **Blooket**: 500K → 15M+ MAU in 2y (secondary sources, flag).

## Jewish institutional network (audience math, US)
- Day schools: **906 schools, ~292K students** (AVI CHAI census 2018–19; no newer
  full census; post-Oct-7 enrollment surge per Prizmah).
- **Hillel: 850+ campuses, ~180–200K Jewish students/yr.**
- **Jewish camps: 300+ camps, ~181K campers/staff (2023 record).**
- **Chabad: ~5,000 centers, 100 countries.**
- **PJ Library: 620K+ subscriber families, 7M+ books/yr** — proof the diaspora
  opts into recurring Jewish cultural content at massive scale.
- Haggadot.com: 500K+ users building custom haggadahs (2021). Chabad.org free
  Haggadah: hundreds of thousands of downloads.

## Discord/Reddit infrastructure
- Discord: 200M+ MAU, 90% play games; 10K+ game communities, 80M+ members.
  Delta Force server: +8–10K members/day at peak, 30–50% retention (Discord's
  own numbers, flag).
- r/WebGames ≈ 135K members; r/incremental_games ≈ 180K.

## Holiday-moment spikes (the calendar is a growth engine)
- Maccabeats "Candlelight": ~1M views in week one of Hanukkah 2010 → 17M+ now;
  made for one community, went global.
- Adam Sandler Chanukah Song: re-charted 4 separate years — holiday content
  re-spikes annually for free.
- 90%+ of top-100-grossing games run seasonal events; ~16% holiday activity
  lift, ~20% engagement lift from event promos (vendor stats, flag).
- Purim costumes Israel: ₪126M market (2024) — Purim is a paying cultural moment.
- No clean Hanukkah-only e-commerce number exists (bundled into $257.8B US
  winter-holiday online spend, flag).
