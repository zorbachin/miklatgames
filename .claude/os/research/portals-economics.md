# Research: web-game portal economics (2025–2026, sourced)

## Poki
- Revshare: **100% to dev for direct traffic** (your own links/community), **50/50 when the player arrives via Poki**. (developers.poki.com/guide/monetization)
- Ads-only (no IAP), hand-curated, every game personally reviewed, 1–2 week review, staged levels + Final Review. Most selective major portal. (sdk.poki.com)
- Scale: ~100M MAU, 1B+ gameplays/month (2025), 1,500+ titles, 500–600 devs.
- Earnings: realistic first-game **$500–$3K/mo** (indiegamebusiness.com); top studios up to **$1M/yr** (Dutch Games Assoc via dealroom); solo dev 67M gameplays in 2025 (medium/@playrea); Blumgi 100M players in 2y.
- Payment via wire/PayPal; cadence unpublished (flag).

## CrazyGames
- Revshare % **not public** (per-partner, rumored ~50%, unverified). Ad share via their SDK only; IAP invite-only via Xsolla.
- **+50% revshare bonus for 2-month launch exclusivity** (1.5× multiplier). (docs.crazygames.com/faq, webgamedev.com/publishing/portals)
- Payouts: monthly, **€100 min**, contractually NET 60 / in practice ~NET 10, wire or PayPal via Tipalti.
- Scale: 40M MAU claimed (35M announced Jun 2024); ~109M visits/mo (Similarweb May 2026); new games get **48 guaranteed homepage hours**.
- Dev datapoint: DonislawDev, 8 small games, 451K plays → €557 total ≈ **€1.2 per 1,000 plays** (small sample, likely pre-2024, flag).

## GameMonetize
- **45% to dev; 90% if you also host it yourself** (dev+publisher stack). NET 30, $30 min, PayPal/USDT. (gamemonetize.com/developers)

## GameDistribution (Azerion)
- **33% of net to dev**, ~NET 60, €100 threshold. Mandatory SDK. Repeated dev complaints about delayed payments (html5gamedevs forum, flag).

## Others
- **Y8**: 50% AdSense share (+10% multiplayer, +5% featured).
- **Lagged**: 50% AdSense share, ~20M reach.
- **Arkadium**: 75% share. **Gameflare**: 50%.
- **Coolmath Games**: one-time non-exclusive **license fee**; anecdotes ~$2,200/game (gameplaydev.substack). No ads/violence; "thinking" games.
- **Armor Games**: historical sponsorships $400–$800 entry, ~$2K avg, $12–20K established (mostly Flash-era figures, flag).
- **Newgrounds**: revshare exists, % unpublished, "tiny amounts."

## Ad economics context
- Web-game banner eCPM ~**$1.50**; rewarded video **$15–25** (sonamine.com); general HTML5 eCPM **$2–6** (playgama 2026).
- Combined Poki+CrazyGames ≈ **95M players/month** (HN, Dec 2024).

**Flags:** CrazyGames base %, Poki payout cadence, GameDistribution real eCPM,
Newgrounds %, all flat-fee figures = anecdotal/dated.
