# Research: tips, PWYW, premium, merch (sourced)

## The convergent number
Across itch PWYW, Helltaker DLC, and Kongregate: **~1–2% of free players ever
pay; average voluntary payment ~$3.50–5.** Plan with this.

## Tip jars
- **Ko-fi: 0% platform fee on tips** (only ~2.9%+$0.30 processing). 5% on
  memberships/shop unless Gold ($12/mo, was $6 — verify). → Ko-fi beats BMC for pure tips.
- **Buy Me a Coffee: 5% fee** + processing. Average donation **≈$5**. Creators
  earned $100M+ in 2022.
- No platform publishes visitor→tipper conversion (flag); proxy = 1–2% of payers-of-anything.

## itch.io
- Open revshare, default **10%** to itch. Payout min **$5**, PayPal/Payoneer.
- PWYW (official 2015 data, only set published): 30% of money is "extra";
  avg donation on free content **$3.68**.
- Hive Time case: 40K downloads → <600 paid ≈ **1–1.5% conversion**; ~20% lost to fees.

## Premium / ad-free unlocks
- Stripe 2.9%+$0.30 (+1.5% intl). Lemon Squeezy / Paddle (merchant of record,
  they handle VAT): ~5%+$0.50 all-in.
- Kongregate canonical web dataset: **2.1% of registered users ever paid**;
  ~4% of payers (whales) = ~half of revenue (2011–15 era, flag).
- Helltaker: free game, $9.99 supporter DLC → **~1.1% attach**, ~$370K gross.

## Merch (print-on-demand)
- Printful/Printify tee: ~$8–13 base → **~$7–8 profit at $20–25 retail (30–40% margin)**.
- Redbubble default markup nets **~$2–4/shirt** minus tier fees (Standard tier 50% fee — flag).

## Web → Steam wrapping (the big-prize path)
- Vampire Survivors: free itch web game → $2.99 Steam → ~$7M first month of 1.0,
  lifetime est. $24–57M (estimates, flag), 27M+ players.
- Cookie Clicker: free web → $4.99 Steam ≈ ~$21.9M gross est.
- Friday Night Funkin': free Newgrounds → Kickstarter **$2.25M from 58,561 backers**.
- Steam wishlist→sale conversion 2024: ~0.17 sales/wishlist at launch.
