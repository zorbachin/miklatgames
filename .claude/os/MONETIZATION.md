# 💰 Miklat Games — Monetization Plan (plan of record)

_Owner: Claude as COO/CMO. Zorba approves; Claude executes._
_Adopted 2026-06-11. Revisit every 2 weeks against the KPIs below._

## Where we actually are (no fantasy numbers)

- **Product:** LIVE — miklatgames.fun arcade with two finished games
  (Iron Dome, Mamad Dash), offline PWA, share/challenge loops already built.
- **Audience:** ~zero. GoatCounter pageviews wired; per-game events not yet.
- **Constraint:** posting is the known blocker — the plan cannot depend on
  daily manual posting to work.

**Therefore the strategy: distribution-first.** Revenue is downstream of
players, so we prioritize channels that *bring their own traffic* (game
portals) over channels that need an audience we don't have yet (ads, merch).

## North star

- **Day 7:** revenue switch is ON (tip jar live) + games submitted to portals.
- **Day 30:** first $100/mo run-rate (portal rev-share + tips).
- **Day 90:** $500–1,000/mo (portals scaling + 1 sponsor or merch drop).

## The phases

### P0 — Flip the revenue switch (this week, ~1 day of work)
1. **Tip jar** — Buy Me a Coffee (or Ko-fi) link on the arcade shelf + each
   game-over screen: "Keep the shelter open ☕". Zero risk, ships in an hour.
   - _Human step:_ Zorba creates the BMC account (5 min). Everything else is Claude.
2. **Per-game GoatCounter events** (already queued) — plays, deaths, shares,
   tip-clicks. We can't run P1/P2 decisions without this.

### P1 — Distribution that pays (weeks 1–4) ← the real wedge
3. **Portal submissions** — package both games for:
   - **itch.io** (instant publish, donations enabled, zero gatekeeping),
   - **CrazyGames + Poki** (curated; rev-share per play; they bring millions
     of players — this is the most attainable *recurring* revenue for HTML5
     games). Claude prepares portal builds (their SDK hooks, no external
     links per their rules) + all submission copy; Zorba's only step is
     account signup.
4. **The viral loop we already built** — challenge cards + "Share with the
   Group Chat" are in the product. Marketing job: 2–3 gameplay clips/week
   (TikTok/IG Reels), produced in the weekly batch block, pre-captioned by
   Claude, Zorba pastes + publishes. Published = done.

### P2 — On-site revenue (when we see ~300+ plays/day)
5. **Direct sponsorship > programmatic ads.** The siren/shelter theme will
   fight AdSense brand-safety filters; an Israeli brand sponsoring the
   coping-humor arcade is a better story and a better CPM. Deliverable:
   one-pager + 10-target outreach list (Claude drafts, Zorba sends).
6. **Merch micro-drop** — 3 designs, print-on-demand (Fourthwall/Printful),
   zero inventory. Only after traffic proves the meme resonates.

### P3 — Product & B2B (quarter 2)
7. **Paid unlocks** — cosmetic skins / supporter pack via Stripe Payment
   Links (no accounts, no backend).
8. **Branded mini-games as a service** — Miklat becomes the live portfolio
   proof for "playable marketing" engagements sold through the consultancy.
   This is the bridge from $1k/mo arcade money to real B2B contracts.

## What we will NOT do (so it stays attainable)

- No AdSense/programmatic ads before P2 traffic — pennies + brand-safety fights.
- No in-game currency/economy builds — heavy lift, kills momentum.
- No new games until both live games are on portals (finish > start).
