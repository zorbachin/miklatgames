# Miklat Academy — curriculum design (internal)

**Format:** one interactive HTML page (`academy/index.html`) in the house
style — it IS a Miklat game: 7 levels, XP bar, missions, boss fight.
localStorage progress. ~2h total, every level ends with a mission on the
REAL venture, not homework-flavored theory.

**Learner profile (Zorba):** ADHD — one screen, one next action; gamified
beats prose; concrete numbers beat concepts; missions must be shippable in
<15 min; the known blocker is publishing (insecurity not ability), so the
course makes *publishing itself* an XP mechanic.

## Levels (≈2h)
- **L0 · The Map (5m)** — how free web games make money AT ALL; the funnel
  (plays → retained players → revenue events); why tribe games get to cheat
  the funnel. The 10M ladder: 1K → 10K → 100K → 1M → 10M, one metric per rung.
- **L1 · The Money (18m)** — every revenue stream ranked for Miklat with real
  numbers: portal revshare, web ad CPMs, tip jar conversion, sponsorship,
  licensing (schools/camps/orgs), merch, premium. Output: their personal
  revenue stack order.
- **L2 · The Tribe (18m)** — identity marketing. Wordle effect; "support your
  tribe" psychology (post-Oct-7 buy-Israeli data); the Jewish calendar as a
  built-in content calendar; WhatsApp as Israel's viral railroad.
- **L3 · Distribution (18m)** — where 10M players actually come from:
  portals, short-form clips, WhatsApp groups, diaspora press, schools/camps,
  Reddit. The 80/20 for a 5-game arcade.
- **L4 · The Experience Moat (15m)** — retention loops that make people pick
  Miklat over Subway Surfers: streaks, challenge links (already built!),
  community leaderboards, holiday events. Session design = ad inventory.
- **L5 · The Brand (12m)** — what Miklat stands for (resilience + humor +
  home), one-roof arcade strategy, voice, why brand = pricing power for
  sponsorships.
- **L6 · The 90-Day Playbook (20m)** — week-by-week plan wired to the org
  (commercialization CEO owns it), KPI ladder, which revenue stream switches
  on at which rung. Ends in BOSS FIGHT: 10-question final + "ship tonight"
  mission.

## Gamification spec
- XP: quiz answers 10–20, missions 50–100 (publishing missions worth double).
- Rank ladder: Shuk Rookie → Stall Owner → Market Boss → Tribe Builder →
  10M General.
- Progress + answers persist in localStorage; confetti on level clear.
- Every level: 1 screen of teaching, 1 quiz, 1 mission. No scrolling walls.
